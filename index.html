About

Starry Heaven Studio is conveniently located in the very heart of Kyiv. Escape the rush of everyday life and visit the place where you can feel free and relaxed.

For beginners

We welcome all people regardless of their age and dance level. Dance is for everyone! Even if you are a beginner, you will find classes suitable for you. Join us and you will become one of the stars in our starry studio.
