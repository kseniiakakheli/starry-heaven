Our short story

In 2018, student Kseniia suggested her own dream of founding a dance studio. Create space where artists will feel themselves confident and inspired. So, ready for changes and recognized that her skills, expertise, and passion would let her turn dream into reality. The Starry Heaven dance studio opened its doors in 2020.
