Classes for beginners

Whether you want to get familiar with the basics of dancing, search for comprehensive instructions to the moves, or just have fun, then this basic dance classes is the ideal class for you! We introduce the basic movements and techniques of each style.

Private lessons

Starry Heaven studio provides private dance lessons tailored to your specific needs.

You will learn the reasons and habits inducing pain and distress in your life, career, family relations or in your overall health. Private dance essons are highly advisable to those who strive to cope with stress, back pain, energy shortage, emotional disbalance, bad posture, and more.
